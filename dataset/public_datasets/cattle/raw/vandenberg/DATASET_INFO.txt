Van den Berg et al. Dataset Structure
=====================================

Dataset DOI: 10.5061/dryad.rq80k
Location: data/public_datasets/raw/vandenberg/

Files:
- Genotypes_26503SNPs.txt (33 MB): Genotype data with 26,503 SNPs
- ID_Breed.txt (11 KB): Breed identifiers for samples

Phenotype Data:
Organized by genetic correlation (GenCor):
- Phenotypes_GenCor_0.4/ (100 replicate files)
- Phenotypes_GenCor_0.8/ (100 replicate files)  
- Phenotypes_GenCor_1/ (100 replicate files)

Each directory contains 100 replicate files:
- Phenotypes_replicate_1.txt
- Phenotypes_replicate_2.txt
- ...
- Phenotypes_replicate_100.txt

Note: The QTL scenario information (3, 30, 300, 3000) may be encoded in the 
replicate numbers or file contents. Further investigation needed to map 
replicates to QTL scenarios.

Total files: 1 genotype + 1 ID + 300 phenotype replicates = 302 files
